ABOUT US

The Team

Founded in 2014, the Techno Wolves is a student run FIRST Robotics Competition team based in Raleigh, North Carolina. Although having recently transitioned into a community-based organization, our 50-student club still maintains a strong relationship with Wake STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) Early College High School. We not only plan on building an awesome robot, but also aim to build a better future by enriching our community through STEM skills and the core values of FIRST, such as Coopertition® and Gracious Professionalism®.

Our Mission

“To enrich the minds of high school students through STEM and robotics in order to promote innovative learning through our partnership with local businesses, schools, and mentors."
