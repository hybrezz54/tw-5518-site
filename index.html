About Us

Techno Wolves Team 5518 is a two year old FIRST Robotics Competition team established in 2014 and based in Raleigh, North Carolina. Our goal is not only to build an awesome robot, but also to build a better future by enriching our schools and communities through STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) and the values of FIRST such as Coopertition® and Gracious Professionalism®.
